# Rock

::: callout tip
The home base for all things rock music
:::

Rock music is a broad genre that emerged in the 1950s from rock and roll, blues, and country. Known for its electric guitar-driven sound, powerful rhythms, and energetic performances, rock has evolved into countless sub-genres including classic rock, punk, metal, and alternative.

## Sweet Home Alabama

::: card
**Sweet Home Alabama** by Lynyrd Skynyrd (1974)

A Southern rock anthem with one of the most recognisable guitar riffs in music history. Originally written as a response to Neil Young's criticism of the South, it became an enduring symbol of regional pride.
:::

## Bohemian Rhapsody

::: card
**Bohemian Rhapsody** by Queen (1975)

A six-minute operatic rock epic that defied every convention of the era. Freddie Mercury's extraordinary composition blends ballad, opera, and hard rock into an unforgettable masterpiece.
:::

## Smells Like Teen Spirit

::: card
**Smells Like Teen Spirit** by Nirvana (1991)

The defining anthem of the grunge movement and Generation X. Kurt Cobain's raw energy and the song's explosive dynamics changed the direction of rock music forever.
:::

## Stairway to Heaven

::: card
**Stairway to Heaven** by Led Zeppelin (1971)

Widely regarded as one of the greatest rock songs ever written, this epic eight-minute track builds from a delicate acoustic intro to a thunderous climax, showcasing the band's extraordinary musical range.
:::
