# Rap

::: callout tip
The home base for all things rap music
:::

::: callout info
🎵 **Songs listed:** 4
:::

Rap is a vocal style and music genre built on rhythm, rhyme, and storytelling. Originating as part of the hip-hop movement, rap has evolved into a global art form spanning countless regional styles, from East Coast lyricism to Southern trap and West Coast G-funk.

## Miami

::: card
**Miami** by Will Smith (1998)

A fun, feel-good rap track celebrating Miami's vibrant culture. Will Smith's charismatic delivery and the infectious Latin-influenced beat made it a summer anthem.
:::

## Rap God

::: card
**Rap God** by Eminem (2013)

A technical tour de force showcasing Eminem's jaw-dropping speed and lyrical dexterity. It set a Guinness World Record for the most words in a hit single.
:::

## m.A.A.d city

::: card
**m.A.A.d city** by Kendrick Lamar feat. MC Eiht (2012)

A vivid and intense portrait of life in Compton, California. Kendrick's storytelling and the track's dramatic beat switch make it one of the most gripping rap songs of the decade.
:::

## All Falls Down

::: card
**All Falls Down** by Kanye West feat. Syleena Johnson (2004)

A soulful and introspective rap track from Kanye's debut album _The College Dropout_, critiquing consumerism and insecurity with heartfelt honesty.
:::
