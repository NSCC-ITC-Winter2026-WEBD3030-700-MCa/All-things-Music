# Hip-Hop

::: callout tip
The home base for all things hip-hop music
:::

::: callout info
🎵 **Songs listed:** 4
:::

Hip-hop is a cultural movement and music genre that emerged in the South Bronx in the 1970s. Built on four pillars — DJing, MCing, breakdancing, and graffiti — hip-hop has grown into one of the most dominant and influential music genres in the world.

## Parents Just Don't Understand

::: card
**Parents Just Don't Understand** by DJ Jazzy Jeff & The Fresh Prince (1988)

A Grammy Award-winning comedic hip-hop classic featuring Will Smith rapping about the relatable struggles of being a teenager. A cornerstone of old-school hip-hop.
:::

## Lose Yourself

::: card
**Lose Yourself** by Eminem (2002)

The Oscar-winning anthem from the film _8 Mile_, capturing the intensity and determination of seizing a single opportunity. One of the greatest hip-hop songs ever recorded.
:::

## HUMBLE.

::: card
**HUMBLE.** by Kendrick Lamar (2017)

A Grammy Award-winning hip-hop track with a minimalist beat and razor-sharp lyricism. Kendrick's confrontational style and cultural commentary made this an instant classic.
:::

## Empire State of Mind

::: card
**Empire State of Mind** by Jay-Z feat. Alicia Keys (2009)

A triumphant anthem celebrating New York City, blending Jay-Z's confident lyricism with Alicia Keys' soaring chorus. One of hip-hop's most iconic tracks.
:::
