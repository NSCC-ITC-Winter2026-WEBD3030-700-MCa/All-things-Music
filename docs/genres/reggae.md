# Reggae
Reggae music originated in Jamaica and is characterized by a strong rhythm, offbeat accents, and socially conscious lyrics.

::: callout tip
The home base for all things reggae music
:::

This page records reggae songs that celebrate life, love, and social issues, spreading messages of peace and unity.
