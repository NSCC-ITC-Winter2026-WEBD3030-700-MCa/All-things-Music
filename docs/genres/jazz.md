# Jazz

::: callout tip
The home base for all things jazz music
:::

::: callout info
🎵 **Songs listed:** 4
:::

Jazz is an American music genre that originated in New Orleans in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Characterised by improvisation, swing, and complex harmonies, jazz has branched into countless sub-genres including bebop, fusion, smooth jazz, and beyond.

## Round Midnight

::: card
**Round Midnight** by Thelonious Monk (1944)

One of the most recorded jazz standards ever written, Monk's moody and introspective composition perfectly captures the atmosphere of late-night jazz clubs.
:::

## So What

::: card
**So What** by Miles Davis (1959)

The opening track of _Kind of Blue_ — the best-selling jazz album of all time — "So What" is a modal jazz masterpiece defined by its cool, relaxed groove.
:::

## Take Five

::: card
**Take Five** by The Dave Brubeck Quartet (1959)

One of the best-selling jazz singles of all time, written in an unusual 5/4 time signature. Paul Desmond's alto saxophone melody is immediately recognisable worldwide.
:::

## A Love Supreme

::: card
**A Love Supreme** by John Coltrane (1964)

A four-part spiritual suite considered one of the greatest jazz recordings ever made. Coltrane's deeply personal and transcendent performance elevated jazz to new artistic heights.
:::
