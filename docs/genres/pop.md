# Pop

::: callout tip
The home base for all things pop music
:::

::: callout info
🎵 **Songs listed:** 4
:::

Pop music is a genre characterised by catchy melodies, relatable lyrics, and broad commercial appeal. Constantly evolving, it draws influences from nearly every other genre and dominates charts around the world.

## Blinding Lights

::: card
**Blinding Lights** by The Weeknd (2020)

A synth-pop masterpiece with 80s-inspired production that broke multiple chart records, spending more weeks in the Billboard Hot 100 top five than any song in history.
:::

## Shape of You

::: card
**Shape of You** by Ed Sheeran (2017)

A tropical pop hit that became one of the best-selling singles of all time, blending acoustic guitar with dancehall rhythms and an irresistibly catchy hook.
:::

## Bad Guy

::: card
**Bad Guy** by Billie Eilish (2019)

A darkly playful pop track from Billie Eilish's debut album, produced by her brother FINNEAS. Its minimalist bass-driven sound and witty lyrics made it a global phenomenon.
:::

## As It Was

::: card
**As It Was** by Harry Styles (2022)

A melancholic yet upbeat synth-pop track that became one of the most streamed songs of 2022, reflecting themes of isolation and longing with an irresistible energy.
:::
