# Drums
Drums shape tempo, energy, and feel, making them essential in genres from rock and jazz to hip-hop and pop.

## Role in music
- Establishes the beat and keeps time for the group.
- Adds dynamics and transitions between song sections.
- Creates groove patterns that define genre style.
