---
title: "Violin"
description: "A bowed string instrument known for expressive tone and melodic range."
---

The violin is a major voice in orchestral and solo performance.

- **Role in music:** Carries lyrical melodies, countermelodies, and emotional dynamics in ensembles and solo works.
- **Notable artists:** Niccolò Paganini, Itzhak Perlman, Hilary Hahn.
