# Violin
The violin is known for its expressive tone and agility, making it a standout melodic instrument.

## Role in music
- Carries lyrical melodies in orchestral and chamber settings.
- Adds emotional texture to film, folk, and crossover music.
- Supports harmony and counter-melody in ensemble writing.

## Notable artists
- Niccolò Paganini
- Itzhak Perlman
- Lindsey Stirling
