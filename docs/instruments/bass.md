# Bass
The bass anchors songs by locking in with drums while outlining chord movement.

## Role in music
- Provides low-end foundation and supports harmonic progression.
- Bridges rhythm and melody through groove-focused lines.
- Helps shape the mood and depth of an arrangement.

## Notable artists
- Jaco Pastorius
- Paul McCartney
- Esperanza Spalding
