# Piano
The piano is a core instrument in classical, jazz, pop, and film music because it can play melody and harmony at the same time.

## Role in music
- Builds harmonic structure with chords and arpeggios.
- Supports songwriting and arrangement through full-range accompaniment.
- Leads both solo performances and ensemble pieces.

## Notable artists
- Ludwig van Beethoven
- Herbie Hancock
- Alicia Keys
