---
title: "Guitar"
description: "A versatile string instrument central to rhythm, harmony, and melody across many genres."
---

The guitar is one of the most widely used instruments in modern music.

- **Role in music:** Provides chord progressions, rhythmic strumming, riffs, and lead solos in styles from folk to rock and jazz.
- **Notable artists:** Jimi Hendrix, B.B. King, Eric Clapton.
