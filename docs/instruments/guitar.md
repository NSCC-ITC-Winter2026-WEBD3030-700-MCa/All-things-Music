# Guitar
The guitar is one of the most flexible instruments in modern music, used for rhythm, harmony, and lead melodies.

## Role in music
- Provides chord progressions and groove in pop, rock, folk, and blues.
- Supports solos and expressive lead lines in rock and jazz.
- Can be played acoustically for intimate arrangements or amplified for powerful live sound.

## Notable artists
- Jimi Hendrix
- Eric Clapton
- Carlos Santana
