# Artists
This page celebrates influential musicians and gives quick insight into their lives and legacy.

## Hall of Fame

### Aretha Franklin
- **Born:** 1942, Memphis, Tennessee, USA
- **Known for:** Powerful soul and gospel vocals; the “Queen of Soul”
- **Life highlights:** Began singing in church as a child and later became a defining voice in civil rights-era music.

### Bob Marley
- **Born:** 1945, Nine Mile, Jamaica
- **Known for:** Bringing reggae to a global audience
- **Life highlights:** Used music to promote unity, spirituality, and social justice, becoming an international cultural icon.

### Freddie Mercury
- **Born:** 1946, Stone Town, Zanzibar (now Tanzania)
- **Known for:** Lead vocalist of Queen and extraordinary stage presence
- **Life highlights:** Combined theatrical performance with vocal range and helped create some of rock’s most memorable songs.

### Whitney Houston
- **Born:** 1963, Newark, New Jersey, USA
- **Known for:** Exceptional vocal control and crossover pop/R&B success
- **Life highlights:** Started in church music and became one of the best-selling artists in music history.

### Fred Durst
- **Born:** 1970, Jacksonville, Florida, USA
- **Known for:** Frontman of Limp Bizkit and a key voice in nu metal
- **Life highlights:** Rose to prominence in the late 1990s by blending rap and heavy rock, helping define a major era of alternative music.

### Gerard Way
- **Born:** 1977, Summit, New Jersey, USA
- **Known for:** Lead vocalist of My Chemical Romance and emo/alternative rock influence
- **Life highlights:** Helped shape 2000s alternative culture through concept-driven albums and emotionally expressive songwriting.

### Crazy Frog
- **Debut:** 2003, Sweden
- **Known for:** Novelty dance single "Axel F" and ringtone-era pop culture impact
- **Life highlights:** Evolved from a viral animated character into an international chart act, symbolizing early internet and mobile-music culture.

### Daft Punk
- **Formed:** 1993, Paris, France
- **Known for:** Pioneering French house sound and blending electronic music with pop and disco influences
- **Life highlights:** The duo (Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo) reshaped modern electronic music and achieved global success with albums like *Discovery* and *Random Access Memories*.
