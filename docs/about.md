# About All-things-Music

## What is All-things-Music?

All-things-Music is a community-driven website that documents a wide variety of songs across many different genres. It provides a friendly experience that allows users to easily explore and discover a diverse range of music.

## Our Goal

::: card Here at All-things-Music, our goals are to:

* Create a welcoming collaborative environment
* Document our community's favourite songs
* Share favourites within the the community
* Introduce a more diverse musical experience to our users
:::

## Why we Do What we Do

We believe that by recording favourite songs within our community, our users will discover a far wider variety of music than they otherwise would.

## Contributing

If you would like to add to All-things-Music's collection of songs, visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/NSCC-ITC-Winter2026-WEBD3030-700-MCa/All-things-Music) and follow the listed contribution guidelines.
