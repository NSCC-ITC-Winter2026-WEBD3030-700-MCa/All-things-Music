# 🎵 Did You Know?

A collection of fascinating, unusual, and mind‑blowing music facts.

---

## 🎧 The First Music Streaming Service Was Invented in 1897
The **Théâtrophone** let people listen to live opera over telephone lines — basically Spotify before electricity was common.

---

## 🎸 The Most Expensive Instrument Ever Sold Cost $15.9 Million
The **“Lady Blunt” Stradivarius** violin sold for nearly $16M and is so valuable it’s rarely played.

---

## 🥁 The Longest Concert Lasted 453 Hours
A Canadian band played for **19 straight days**, rotating members to keep the music going.

---

## 🎤 Humans Can Sing Notes Lower Than a Tuba
The world record for lowest vocal note is **0.189 Hz**, far below what humans can normally hear.

---

## 🎼 The Oldest Known Song Is Over 3,400 Years Old
Called the **Hurrian Hymn**, it was discovered on clay tablets in ancient Syria.

---

## 🎹 Beethoven Composed His Ninth Symphony While Nearly Deaf
He couldn’t hear the orchestra — he felt the vibrations through the floor.

---

## 🎧 The Loudest Concert Ever Reached 136 dB
KISS once played so loud that fans reported **permanent hearing damage**.

---

## 🎤 “Happy Birthday” Used to Be Copyrighted
Restaurants avoided singing it to dodge licensing fees until the copyright was overturned in 2016.

---

## 🎸 The World’s Largest Playable Guitar Is 43 Feet Long
It weighs over 2,000 pounds and requires **crane‑sized picks** to play.
